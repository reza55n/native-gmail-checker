Prerequisites

To see unread status on tab's icon: Go to Gmail's all settings > Advanced, and enable 'Unread message icon and badge'

To prevent deactivate Gmail's tab: Go to Chrome's settings > Performance > Always keep these sites active and add 'mail.google.com'
